Impact to Mental Health

While not definitivley caused by the excessive usage of AI, there has been strong correlations made between frequent AI ussage and harmful social factors such as increased need for affiliation and lonliness. In some studies, it has been found that even moderate dependancy on AI chatbots is correlated to higher levels of depression compared to non-users.

However, it is important to consider whether the AI is actually a factor in the depression or if depression leads to more AI usage. Either way, I firmly believe that we should be cautious of AI replacing our peers and social interactions. I personally would not want to live in a world where AI is expected to be a daily source of human interaction.
