Why make a website about AI?

As listed above, I am a computer science major at the University of Nebraska Omaha. I often times deal with AI in this major, and as a student I see many examples of people using AI to complete their work. I personally use some AI tools such as the one built into PyCharm, but otherwise try to avoid larger AI involvement such as ChatGPT. This has inspired me to look deeper into what a dependancy on AI can cause academically. When researching, I ended up finding multiple papers about the link between AI and multiple adverse affects. I hope to spread awareness to this a little bit and advocate for safe AI usage.
